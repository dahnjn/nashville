How to Play

Claim the board. Connect your sides. Race to 50.

1

Place hexagons

On your turn, choose an open hexagon on the board. Your team color will claim that space.

2

Capture territory

Surround a hexagon on four edges to capture it for your team.

3

Connect your sides

The first team to create a connected path between its assigned sides wins immediately.

4

Or reach 50

The first team to place or control 50 hexagons also wins immediately.

The scoreboard tracks each team’s current hex total. If nobody connects sides first, the race to 50 decides the game.
