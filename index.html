RULES

Each team tries to connect its two matching-color sides: Blue, Red, or Yellow.

The turn rhythm is alive: everyone takes 1 move, then everyone takes 2 moves, then everyone takes 3 moves, then it repeats.

Common hexes connect through every team, but cannot be claimed. Corner bases split to show the two teams that can use that edge as a goal.

A surrounded hex is captured automatically when a team controls 4 of its 6 edges.

Each team has one ◈ move. Click an opponent hex and confirm to make it a common hex. It counts as a move.

First team to connect its two goal sides wins. First team to control 50 hexes also wins. Whichever happens first ends the game.
